Tools for running this research without a paid subscription. Each one serves a specific function in the biweekly workflow.

VidIQ Chrome ExtensionFree Browser Extension
Primary Tool — Install First
What It Does
Overlays real-time view counts, subscriber stats, engagement rates, and keyword scores directly on YouTube channel pages and video listings as you browse. No manual lookups needed.
How to Use It
Install the extension, then navigate to any channel (Feminine Universe, Erin Lyons TV, etc.) and sort by Most Popular. View counts and engagement data appear inline on every video thumbnail.
Why It Matters
This is the tool that solves the problem of not being able to see top video performance without paid access. Free tier gives you enough for this research cadence.
Biweekly Workflow
Browse channel → sort by Most Popular → log the top 5 videos with view counts into the Biweekly Log tab.

YouTube StudioFree — Built In
Benchmark Your Own Content
What It Does
Access your own video analytics — impressions, click-through rate, average view duration, and audience retention curves. Lets you compare your content's performance against adjacent channels.
How to Use It
Check after every video posts. Look at the retention curve to see exactly where viewers drop off. Cross-reference with what adjacent channels are doing on the same topic.
Why It Matters
Most creators check views but ignore the retention curve. That curve tells you which section of your script needs work — and which sections are your strongest.
Biweekly Workflow
After each research pass, pull your top 3 videos by watch time and note what topics and structures they share. That's your signal for what to produce more of.

YouTube Search — Incognito ModeFree — No Account Needed
Keyword & Title Research
What It Does
Search YouTube in an incognito window to see unbiased autocomplete suggestions — what the algorithm surfaces to a cold viewer, not someone already in your orbit.
How to Use It
Type partial phrases your audience would search: "why do smart women," "women who overthink," "feminine energy and," "self-worth and." Screenshot the autocomplete. These are real search behaviors.
Why It Matters
Autocomplete is free keyword research. The phrases YouTube suggests are pulled from actual search volume. Any phrase that autocompletes is a topic with proven demand.
Biweekly Workflow
Run one incognito search pass per biweekly cycle. Choose 3 seed phrases relevant to your current content focus and log the autocomplete results.

Social BladeFree Tier Available
Channel Growth Tracking
What It Does
Tracks subscriber and view count growth over time for any YouTube channel. Shows trajectory — growing, plateauing, or declining — and surfaces historical upload patterns.
How to Use It
Search any adjacent channel by name. Free tier shows monthly subscriber and view trends. Look for channels growing despite low upload frequency — that signals evergreen content worth studying.
Why It Matters
A channel with 319K subscribers posting 0.5 videos/week and still gaining views (like The Feminine Universe) is producing evergreen content. That upload-to-view ratio tells you more than the sub count alone.
Biweekly Workflow
Check once per month, not every biweekly pass. Flag any adjacent channel showing accelerating growth and add them to the Channel Landscape tab.
